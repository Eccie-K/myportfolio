THE PROCESS

HOW IT WORKS

There is a layed out and detailed
process for creating engaging
applications that work...

1. Understanding

Understanding the problem,the business, the customer, the value proposition and how success will be determined.

2. Diverging

Generating insights and potential solutions.Exploring as many ways as possible of solving the problem.

3. Converging

Choosing feasible ideas that guide the impelementation of the prototype to be tested with exisiting or potential customers

4. Prototyping

Building a prototype to be tested with exisiting or potential customers to learn about specific unknowns and assumptions.

5. Testing and Learning

Testing the protype with existing or potential customers to help understand what is needed.
